About

I am Joshua R. McGregor a software engineer and technological enthusiast. I have expressed my interest in technology since I was very young by disassembling/reassembling old computers and other pieces of tech. This led to me slowly gaining more and more experience until a family friend said they would pay me if I could get their computer working. I realized that people needed my expertise and knowledge, so I continued learning. I built my own computers, sped up old systems, reconfigured networks, then I turned my sights to web development.

Learning web development led me to so much more then I could have imagined. I was able to build websites, modify existing ones, and understand how websites and the internet at its core functioned. This crafted website you are reading serves as both an explanation and example of my experience in software engineering.

If you would like to know more about what I do visit the 'Work' tab.
